Club Chicken Pasta Salad

This is a refreshing and hearty pasta salad perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

    8 ounces rotini pasta
  • 1/2 cup salad dressing
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Muenster cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh herbs for garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
  2. Drain and rinse the pasta under cold water; drain again.
  3. Combine the pasta, salad dressing, and mayonnaise in a large bowl; stir to coat.
  4. Add the chicken, bacon, tomatoes, Muenster cheese, celery, green bell pepper, and avocado; stir to combine.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.

Enjoy your Club Chicken Pasta Salad!

Feel free to customize the ingredients to your liking!
